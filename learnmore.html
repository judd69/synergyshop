About Synergy

Our Commitment

At Synergy, we are committed to build a better relationship between the consumer and retailers by understanding and knowing the source of the product. We intend to list the manufacturers and display the given rating from other users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make the job of the manufacturer as well as the retailers job easier. There wont be any hassle for the retailers in talking to third party manufacturers in order to sell. It will also be easier to ship directly from the warehouse instead of having it delivered to a physical store.
